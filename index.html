Wind Speed

From looking at this figure, we can see that there is no association between wind speed and latitude. Most cities included remained between 0 and 15mph wind speeds, with a small number between 15 and 20 mph. Although there are a couple of outliers above 25mph, from the available data there is no way to accurately predict what caused them.
